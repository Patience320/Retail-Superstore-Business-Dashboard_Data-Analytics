# Retail Superstore Data Analysis (Interactive Dashboard creation using PowerBI)

## Project Objective: 
The client operates a retail superstore specializing in three main product categories: Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology. The business serves a diverse range of regions and customer segments, focusing on both households and corporate clients.                                                  

## Business Core Mission: 
The company’s primary objective is to supply everyday goods that are accessible, reliable, and offer strong value for money. 
By ensuring products are easy to obtain and consistently dependable, the superstore positions itself as a trusted provider for both home and business needs.      To gain a clearer understanding of overall business performance, the client has requested a comprehensive report and dashboard. This will address key business questions around sales and profit trends, category contributions, customer segments, shipping efficiency, and regional profitability.

## KPIs

-What are the monthly Sales and Profit Trends Over Time?

-Which categories and subcategories contribute the most to Sales and Profit?

-Which segment generates the Highest Revenue and Profit?

-Which shipping mode is mostly used and generates the Most Profit?

-Which states have the Highest and Lowest Profitability?

-Average Delivery Time in Days (ship date - order date) and how does it 
differ by Shipping Mode?

-How do different Ship Mode impacts Shipping Cost and Profit?

## Insights and Analysis

## 📊 Monthly Sales and Profit Trends
•	Sales fluctuate significantly across months, with peaks around March, May, and October (sales above 1.3M–2M).

•	Profit trends are less stable, with some months showing strong margins (March, May) and others weaker (July, September).

•	Overall, sales and profit do not move in perfect sync, suggesting seasonality and cost variations.

## 🏷️ Categories & Sub-Categories Contribution
•	Technology drives the highest sales (0.7M) and profit (4.7M).

•	Furniture contributes less in sales (0.3M) but still yields high profit (4.1M).

•	Office Supplies sits in the middle (0.5M sales, 3.8M profit).

•	Sub-categories like Phones, Copiers, and Chairs dominate sales, while Binders and Paper contribute steadily in Office Supplies.


## 👥 Segment Analysis
•	Consumer segment is the strongest, generating 0.7M revenue and 6.5M profit.

•	Corporate follows with 0.4M revenue and 3.8M profit.

•	Home Office is the smallest contributor (0.3M revenue, 2.3M profit).

•	Insight: The business is consumer-driven, but corporate clients also provide solid profitability.


## 🚚 Shipping Mode & Delivery Time
•	Standard Class is the most used, with the highest profit (~1M) and average delivery time of 5 days.

•	Second Class and First Class are faster (3.23 and 2.18 days respectively) but less profitable.

•	Same Day shipping is fastest (0.04 days) but least profitable (76K).

•	Trade-off: Faster delivery modes reduce profit margins due to higher shipping costs.


## 🌍 Geographic Profitability
•	Top States by Profit: California, New York, England, Ile-de-France, New South Wales.

•	Lowest States by Profit: Texas, Ohio, Istanbul, Lagos, Izmir.

•	Insight: Profitability is concentrated in developed markets, while emerging regions show losses.
